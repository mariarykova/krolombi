about me

London based, Moscow born, Olya is a fashion and personal stylist and consultant. Olya works with private clients, magazines and digital platforms. She started her journey by graduating from UAL where she studied Fashion Styling and Creative Direction.

Olya is experienced in using colour therapy that can be applied in personal styling, wardrobe review and interior design. In her work, she likes to bring a mix of historic references and cultures and combine them with modern trends.

The ingredients of her work are the creation of themes, concept, styling and creative direction. She creates stylish and contemporary images for editorial photoshoots and for private clients.

Olya is available for Editorial, Advertorial, Commercial and Personal Styling.
